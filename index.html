RITUAL — Free Daily Routine Planner & Habit Tracker

Build Better Daily Habits with RITUAL

RITUAL is a free daily routine planner and habit tracker that helps you organize your day, build consistent habits, and track your progress — all without creating an account. Your data is saved locally in your browser, so it works completely offline.

Features of the RITUAL Daily Planner

  • Timeline View — Schedule tasks by time of day with a clean, chronological view. See your entire daily routine at a glance.
  • Habit Tracker & Streak Counter — Track your daily completion streaks and build momentum over time.
  • Weekly Frequency Scheduling — Set tasks to repeat daily or on specific days of the week for fully automated routine building.
  • Stats & Analytics Dashboard — View your average completion rate, perfect days, top categories, and a 16-week activity heatmap.
  • Category Breakdown — Organize tasks across Morning, Work, Health, Evening, Personal, and Learning categories.
  • Mood Tracker — Log how you feel each day with a quick mood selector and daily notes.
  • Level System — Earn levels from "Inactive" to "God-Tier" based on your average task completion rate.
  • Kanban Board View — Switch between timeline and board views for different planning perspectives.
  • HTML Report Export — Download a beautiful progress report with your stats, streak, mood history, and completion data.
  • Dark & Light Mode — Switch between elegant dark and light themes.

How to Use RITUAL as a Daily Schedule Maker

Open RITUAL and start adding tasks using the + Add Task button. Give each task a name, time, duration, category, and priority. Enable daily or weekly repeat to auto-populate future days. Check off tasks as you complete them and watch your streak grow. Navigate between days using the arrow buttons or the week strip in the sidebar.

Why RITUAL is the Best Free Habit Tracker

Unlike most habit trackers and daily planners, RITUAL requires no sign-up and stores all data locally on your device. It combines a beautiful design with powerful productivity features: a timeline planner, kanban board, analytics, heatmap, mood log, and level progression system — completely free, forever.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is RITUAL free to use?

Yes, RITUAL is 100% free. There is no subscription, no premium tier, and no account required.

Does RITUAL work offline?

Yes. RITUAL is a browser-based app that works offline. All your tasks and settings are stored in your browser's local storage.

Can I track habits with RITUAL?

Yes. RITUAL tracks daily and weekly habits, shows your current streak, and displays a 16-week activity heatmap to visualize your consistency.

How do I build a daily routine with RITUAL?

Add tasks with specific times and set them to repeat daily or on chosen days of the week. RITUAL automatically injects recurring tasks into future days so your routine is always ready.
